TikTok Terms and Conditions

What's inside TikTok T&C?

The terms and conditions (T&C) are a series of statements that are usually found before accessing a platform for the first time. Simply put, the T&C clarifies the user's rights and obligations while using the platform. This also serves as a legal contract or agreement between the service provider and the user. Agreeing to the T&C states that the user consents to the platform's system of management before using the site.

Depending on the site's purpose, a platform's T&C varies. In the case of TikTok, their T&C has 7 general sections including:

1. Privacy Policy

States what specific type of data is collected from users. In comparison to the T&C, the privacy policy emphasizes conditions regarding data collected from users.

2. TikTok Platform Cookies Policy

An in-depth section on how cookies are used when using the platform. This includes discussing tracking technologies used by first and third party. Also includes how users can control its use.

3. Childrens Privacy Policy
  • Similar to the privacy policy, this section discusses what data is collected. This mainly discusses data collection from children using the platform.
  • Emphasizes the use of TikTok's 'Kids Mode' that was designed to control the content being viewed by individuals under 13 years of age.
4. Open Source
  • Discusses open source apps and licenses used by the platform.
  • This section gives credit to the software and provides transparency on apps used.
5. Virtual Items
  • A section on the virtual currency purchased on the site. It gives an in-depth explanation of the types of purchases that can be made to support content creators. Available payment methods are also stated here.
  • Emphasizes the purchase method for 'gifts' and 'diamonds' to support content creators.
  • TikTok also notes here that purchases should also be conducted by those 18 years old and above.
6. Intellectual Property Policy
  • States Tiktok's strict policy against copyright infringement. Includes the platform's penalty and strict procedures when users attempt to violate user content.
  • Consequences for violating intellectual property include account termination/suspension
  • To avoid account termination, T&C encourages direct contact with the user. Otherwise, the involved party may file a Copyright Infringement Report on the violating user.
7. Law Enforcement Guidelines

Additional information and requirements are needed for filing legal requests. States how TikTok handles requests from law enforcement authorities.

Importance of Terms and Conditions

  1. Covers all areas

    2. T&C discusses not only liability but also the platform's overview. It states needed commercial terms such as data agreement

  2. Certainty

    3. A clear T&C establishes a solid legal stance. Any violation of the contract can easily be identified and will avoid a legal dispute. The T&C enforces the system and supports the whole platform.

  3. Compliance

    4. With platforms dealing with user data collection, a privacy policy should be included in the T&C as required by the law. The T&C is also used as a legal requirement before any action between the user and service provider is done.

  4. Clarity

    5. The T&C ensures users of the platformâ€™s system. Through the T&C, users are informed of their current situation as a user. It clarifies the service being provided and the rights of a user on the platform.

  5. Confidence and Consistency

    6. For platform handlers, the T&C provides a clear vision of the platform. This allows staff to reevaluate their site and improve it based on client expectations.

Privacy policy introduction

Next to the platform's terms and conditions, one of its most significant parts is the privacy policy.Tiktok provides a very personal user experience due to its detailed process of data collection inside and outside app usage. Personalized ads and content are just a few things that the platform provides for the user.

Similar to the T&C, TikTok provides a detailed outline of everything that involves user data. It includes what data is collected, how data is distributed/collected, why data is collected, and who has access to your data. Additionally, this includes how you can reach the platform in case of complaints or breach of contract in your respective regions.
